The authorities (including the QCB, MOF and QIA) have traditionally lent strong support to banks
during market turbulences and formalizing crisis management frameworks would further enhance
predictability, efficiency and confidence. The QCB should continue to review its macroprudential
policies and adjust as needed to mitigate potential macro-financial risks, which tend to build up
amid easing financial conditions. Building on the QCB's progress in strengthening financial stability,
staff continued to recommend that Qatar undertake an FSAP update (last FSAP was concluded in
2007).
