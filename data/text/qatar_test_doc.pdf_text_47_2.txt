Inclusiveness. Qatar aims at maintaining a high fertility rate (of 3 percent) and increase the 
economic participation of people with disabilities (to 30 percent by 2030). The government will 
launch marriage and family support programs, enhance maternity leave, and promote financial 
literacy. For vulnerable segments, initiatives include better identification, skill development, and 
expanded social services. Women's empowerment and volunteerism (targeting a 10 percent 
volunteer rate) will also be key focuses.
