A big data approach in variable selection is developed to enhance the coverage of potential 
predictors of economic growth and reduces the lag between real-world events and nowcasts. 
Unlike traditional methods that select variables based on specific economic theories, this 
approach collects a large number of variables including those that are unconventional in 
economic analysis, and evaluates their correlations with Qatar’s non-hydrocarbon GDP.
