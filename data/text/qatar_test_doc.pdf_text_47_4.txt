Climate and sustainability. Qatar aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 25 percent 
relative to the business-as-usual scenario by 2030 and expand renewable energy capacity to 
4 GW. The government will protect 30 percent of its land and marine areas, restore 30 percent of 
degraded natural habitats and reduce groundwater extraction by 70 percent, with per-capita 
water consumption below 310 liters per day. Plans include deploying carbon capture 
technologies, expanding biodiversity protection, improving water conservation, and fostering 
circular economy practices.
